ABOUT US

One stop solution for QUALITY garments accessories with VALUE for money in a CONVENIENT way.

SPLENOR is a trims & accessories chain offering a wide and varied selection for all types garments - knit, woven, and sweater. We consider ourselves as environmental friendly garment accessories provider. Our products are mostly made in China as well as Bangladesh.

We provide complete apparel solutions from underwear to outerwear and fashion accessories. Our product range is fully compiled with men, women and kids garments and offer the same to our valued customers all over the world by the personalized service, product quality, competitive price and speed of service. We specialize in laces, buttons, belts, zippers, badges, stoppers, buckles, toggles, velcro, cord end, drawstring, tapes, hit transfer sticker, rhinestones, any kind of sequence work, chiffon/georgette fabric, fur fabrics, frill tape, embroidery work and so on.

We are engaged to maintain a consistent high quality standard of business conduct, ethics and social responsibilities. We take pride in the efficiency of what we do and always try to adopt to the latest and most effective technology available.
